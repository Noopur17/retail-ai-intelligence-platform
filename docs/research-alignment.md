# 🔬 Research & Engineering Alignment

## Overview

This project explores practical AI engineering patterns for modern retail and commerce systems.

The platform connects:

- Recommendation systems
- Generative AI
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Semantic search
- Vector databases
- Dataset-driven AI workflows

---

## Research-Oriented Themes

### 1. Retail Recommendation Systems

The platform includes a recommendation service focused on:

- Product similarity
- Category-aware discovery
- Ranking workflows
- Retail product discovery

---

### 2. Retrieval-Augmented Generation

The RAG assistant explores:

- Knowledge ingestion
- OpenAI embeddings
- ChromaDB vector storage
- Semantic retrieval
- Context-grounded AI responses

---

### 3. AI-Powered Content Intelligence

The content intelligence service explores:

- Product description generation
- SEO metadata creation
- Structured retail content
- Merchandising content workflows

---

### 4. Dataset-Driven AI Systems

The platform connects with a Retail AI Knowledge Base dataset designed for:

- RAG systems
- Semantic search
- Recommendation workflows
- Commerce AI assistants
